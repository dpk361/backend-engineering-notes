## Collections and Recovery 

### What is Collections and Recovery?

Collections and Recovery is the banking process used when:
- a customer does not repay borrowed money on time
- loan EMIs become overdue
- credit card dues are unpaid
- debts become delinquent

The bank then:
- reminds the customer
- follows up for payment
- collects overdue money
- recovers bad loans

---

# Applicable Accounts

Collections and Recovery mainly applies to:

## 1. Loan Accounts

Examples:
- Home loan
- Personal loan
- Car loan
- Education loan
- Business loan

Reason:
- Customer has borrowed money from the bank
- Bank expects repayment with EMI/interest

If payment is missed:
- Collection process starts

---

## 2. Credit Card Accounts

Examples:
- Unpaid credit card bills
- Minimum amount overdue

Reason:
- Customer used bank credit
- Payment is pending

Bank performs:
- reminders
- penalties
- recovery follow-ups

---

## 3. Corporate Loan Accounts

Examples:
- Business loans
- Corporate borrowing
- Working capital loans
- Cash credit accounts

Reason:
- Companies owe money to the bank

If repayment fails:
- recovery/legal process may start

---

# Usually NOT Applicable To

## Savings Accounts

Normally:
- no repayment obligation exists

So collections/recovery is usually not needed.

Example:
- Your salary savings account

---

## Current Accounts

Normally:
- used for transactions only

No loan exists unless:
- overdraft facility is attached

---

## Fixed Deposits (FDs)

FDs are:
- customer deposits into the bank

Bank actually owes money to customer.

So collections process is not applicable.

---

## Nostro Accounts

Nostro accounts belong to:
- banks themselves

Used for:
- international settlements

Collections/recovery is not applicable.

---

# Simple Understanding

Collections & Recovery applies wherever:

> The customer owes money to the bank.

---

# Quick Summary Table

| Account Type | Collections Applicable? | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| Savings Account | Usually No | Customer deposit account |

| Loan Account | Yes | Customer borrowed money |
| Credit Card Account | Yes | Payment due to bank |
| Corporate Loan Account | Yes | Business owes bank |
