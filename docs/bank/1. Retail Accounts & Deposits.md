## Retail Accounts & Retail Deposits

### Retail Accounts
Retail accounts are bank accounts used by individual customers (normal people) for personal banking purposes such as:
- Savings
- Salary deposits
- Fixed deposits
- Personal loans
- Daily transactions

Examples:
- Savings account
- Salary account
- Personal FD account

---

### Retail Deposits
Retail deposits are the money deposited by individual customers into the bank through retail accounts.

Examples:
- Savings account balance
- Fixed Deposits (FDs)
- Recurring Deposits (RDs)

---

### Simple Understanding

- **Retail Account** = The customer’s banking account
- **Retail Deposit** = The money stored in those accounts

Example:

If Mohan keeps:
- ₹50,000 in a savings account
- ₹2 lakhs in an FD

Then:
- The savings account and FD account are called **Retail Accounts**
- The ₹2.5 lakhs kept in the bank are called **Retail Deposits**
