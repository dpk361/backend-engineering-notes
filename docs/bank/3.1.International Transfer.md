## International Transfer Flow Using Nostro Account

Suppose:

- Mohan in India wants to send **$10,000**
- To a person/company having USD account `XYZ` in USA

---

### Step-by-Step Flow

### Step 1 — Mohan’s Account Debited

Money is deducted from:

```text
Mohan Savings Account
        ↓
ICICI Bank India
```

At this stage:
- Mohan pays in INR (or equivalent amount)

---

### Step 2 — ICICI Uses Its Own Funds

ICICI Bank now needs to send USD internationally.

So ICICI uses:
- its own foreign currency holding

This usually happens through:

```text
ICICI Bank's Nostro Account in USA
```

Example:

```text
ICICI USD Nostro Account
maintained in Citibank USA
```

---

### Step 3 — USD Transfer Happens

Now money moves:

```text
ICICI Nostro Account (USD)
            ↓
Receiver USD Account XYZ
```

---

## Complete Simplified Flow

```text
Mohan Account (India)
        ↓
ICICI Bank India
        ↓
ICICI USD Nostro Account (USA)
        ↓
Receiver USD Account XYZ
```

---

## Very Important Understanding

The Nostro account is:

- NOT Mohan’s account
- NOT receiver’s account

It is:
> ICICI Bank’s own foreign currency account maintained abroad.

---

## Simple Analogy

Think like this:

- Mohan gives money to ICICI in India
- ICICI already keeps some USD money in USA
- ICICI uses that USD wallet to pay the receiver

That USD wallet abroad is the Nostro account.
